# Limes Axis Contributor License Agreement Baseline

This document is an initial project baseline. It is not a substitute for legal
review. Limes Labs should have this text reviewed before accepting substantial
external contributions.

## Intent

The purpose of the CLA is to keep the Limes Axis open-source core legally clean
while allowing Limes Labs to operate an open-core business around managed cloud,
enterprise deployment, support and commercial extensions.

## Contributor Confirmation

By submitting a contribution, you confirm that:

- you have the right to submit the contribution;
- the contribution is your original work or you have permission to submit it;
- the contribution does not knowingly violate third-party rights;
- you grant Limes Labs and downstream users the rights needed to use, modify,
  distribute and sublicense the contribution as part of Limes Axis;
- you understand the open-source core is licensed under Apache-2.0 unless a
  different license is explicitly stated for a specific file.

## Corporate Contributions

If you contribute on behalf of an employer or organization, you confirm that you
are authorized to do so or that the organization has approved the contribution.

Corporate contribution terms should be reviewed separately before accepting
material contributions from companies or institutions.

## Future Process

Before accepting broad external contributions, this repository should add:

- reviewed individual CLA text;
- reviewed corporate CLA text;
- a clear signature process;
- GitHub automation or manual verification policy;
- contributor records retention policy.
